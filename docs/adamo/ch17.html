The Normandy had ascended to an orbital position shortly after neutralizing the geth, where it could keep better watch for geth dropships approaching the planet. All of the quarians were grateful for the assistance provided by Shepard and her ship. What Shepard didn't tell the quarians, was that she received the dossier at a late time, when she was already in the Dholen system, approaching Haestrom. No one knew that Shepard's timely arrival was thanks to Tali giving her specific details of the mission.

An upbringing devoid of any real emotional attachment had long resulted in her putting up a hard, carefully controlled outer shell, which prevented her from ever getting very close to anyone—to her, subconsciously, people were unreliable and potential betrayals.
